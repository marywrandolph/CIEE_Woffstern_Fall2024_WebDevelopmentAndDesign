Cappucino...... 70 Kč

A bold espresso shot, delicately topped with a dollop of velvety steamed milk.

Latte...... 90 Kč

A smooth and creamy espresso shot over steamed milk, topped with a light layer of foam.

Flat White...... 90 Kč

A rich and velvety coffee crafted with a bold shot of espresso and a smooth layer of micro-foamed milk.

Mocha...... 100 Kč

A fusion of espresso, steamed milk, and rich chocolate syrup, topped with a touch of whipped cream or a dusting of cocoa powder.

A warm, spicy blend of chai tea and espresso, combined with steamed milk for a rich, creamy texture.
